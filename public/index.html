Welcome, Traveller

The Rebel’s Tavern stands at the edge of a bustling crossroads — a place where adventurers, knights, and common folk alike come to rest, share tales, and raise their mugs to fortune.

Step inside, warm yourself by the fire, and join the other patrons. The barkeep keeps track of every name and coin, and your deeds will shape your reputation across the land.

How It Works

Simply speak in the Twitch chat at twitch.tv/dogoftheoccult to enter the tavern. From that moment on, your gold, health, and actions will be recorded each time you visit.

Tavern Commands

Use these commands in chat to take part in the tavern’s daily life:

CommandDescription
!statusCheck your gold, health, honour, and how drunk you are.
!dailyClaim your 50 gold tavern allowance once every 12 hours.
!questUndertake a short daily adventure to earn gold and honour.
!stealAttempt to pinch a few coins. Careful — the barkeep has sharp eyes.
!diceGamble with the regulars in a game of chance.
!tipbardSpend 5 gold to hear a song and win the crowd’s favour.
!shopView the Tavern Shop for drinks, meals, and trinkets.
!buy [item]Buy an item from the shop (e.g. !buy bread).
!use [item]Consume or use an item you’ve purchased.
!titlesSee all the titles you’ve earned through your actions.
!title [name]Equip a title you’ve unlocked to show your status.

Rise Through the Ranks

The tavern remembers every victory and every misdeed. Earn honour through quests and good deeds to rise from a humble patron to a celebrated legend of the realm.

  • Common Drifter – every traveller starts here
  • Regular – known to the barkeep and trusted with small favours
  • Champion – recognised for deeds and honour
  • Legend – a name sung by bards and feared by cheats

Join the Fellowship

Type anything in Twitch chat to step through the tavern doors. Earn your gold, take your quests, and let the firelight tell your story.
